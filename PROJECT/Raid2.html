RAID 2

Justice will be served

Release Date May 1st, 2025
Genre Action/Adventure
Language Hindi
Director Raj Kumar Gupta
★ 4.5/5

Following the tremendous success of the first film, RAID 2 continues the story of fearless Income Tax officer Amay Patnaik as he takes on another high-profile case of tax evasion and corruption. Based on real events, this action-packed sequel promises even more intense confrontations and daring operations against powerful adversaries who consider themselves above the law.

Synopsis

Set a few years after the events of the first film, RAID 2 follows Amay Patnaik who is now known for his uncompromising attitude and reputation for conducting successful raids against tax evaders. When information about a major political figure involved in large-scale money laundering comes to light, Patnaik must navigate dangerous waters as he plans the biggest raid of his career. With threats to his life and family mounting, Patnaik must stay one step ahead of his powerful enemies while uncovering a conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of power.
