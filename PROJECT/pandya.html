All The Best Pandya

A heartwarming family drama

Release Date April, 2025
Genre Drama
Language Gujarati
Director Krishnadev Yagnik
★ 4.2/5

"All The Best Pandya" (Today's Generation) is a touching Gujarati family drama that explores the generational gap and evolving family dynamics in modern India. The film follows a traditional family trying to adapt to changing times as they navigate relationships, values, and the true meaning of family.

Synopsis

The story revolves around the Mehta family, where the young son Rohan returns home after studying abroad with new perspectives that clash with his traditional father's worldview. As they navigate their differences, unexpected circumstances force them to reconsider their relationship and understanding of each other. Through humor, emotion, and relatable family situations, the film depicts how bridges can be built between generations when people open their hearts and minds.
